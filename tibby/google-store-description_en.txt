Treeppets are out there!!!

An original platform game. A game for the whole family. Free version of Tibby the treeppets hunter.
The ship that is transporting treeppets eggs from the Nursery to Treep planet falls on Earth and little treeppets escape. You are hired to catch them all and bring them back to the ship so that they can return back home.

Help Tibby to find and catch them all.

No advertisements and no in-game purchase, just the Fun!

Good Luck!
